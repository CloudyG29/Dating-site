The Process

How Soulthread Works

We stripped away the swiping and the superficial to focus entirely on deep, intentional connections.

🎭
1. Create an Anonymous Profile
You won't upload photos or use your real name. Instead, you'll share your values, your lifestyle, and exactly what you are looking for in a partner.
🧠
2. The Algorithm Goes to Work
Our backend matching engine compares your core dealbreakers, goals, and lifestyle tags with other active members to find highly compatible pairs.
🔒
3. Mutual Consent
When a match is found, both parties review the anonymous profiles. Your contact details (like phone number) are strictly locked.
🤝
4. The Reveal
Only if both of you click "Accept" does the system reveal your contact details to each other so you can take the conversation into the real world.
